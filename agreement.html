About Recipe Recommendation Agent

Welcome to Recipe Recommendation Agent, your intelligent cooking companion designed to simplify meal planning and enhance your cooking experience. Our system analyzes available ingredients, user preferences, and past interactions to recommend recipes that are both practical and enjoyable.

Whether you are a beginner learning the basics or an experienced cook exploring new cuisines, this platform adapts to your needs by suggesting relevant recipes, generating shopping lists for missing ingredients, and improving recommendations over time using user feedback.

Our mission is to promote smart cooking, reduce food waste, save time and money, and encourage healthier eating habits through technology.
